About LovePDFs

Built for people who deserve free, private tools — with no strings attached.

Our Story

LovePDFs was created out of frustration with PDF tools that require signups, impose file size limits, store your files on servers, and charge for basic features. We believed there had to be a better way — and there is.

By leveraging modern browser technology (PDF.js, pdf-lib, WebAssembly), we built a suite of 43 tools that run entirely on your device. No servers. No accounts. No limits.

What kind of company LovePDFs is

LovePDFs is a practical tools brand focused on privacy-aware document workflows, fast browser utilities, and straightforward product design. We maintain company, legal, support, and blog pages alongside the tools so users can understand who operates the site and how the service works before relying on it.

Founder Story

LovePDFs was founded by Rameez, an independent builder who wanted simple document tools that respected users instead of trapping them behind paywalls. The first versions were built to solve everyday frustrations: oversized PDFs, awkward conversion tools, and sites that forced uploads even for private files.

That founder mindset still shapes the product today. Every new tool is judged on the same principles: does it save time, does it keep files local when possible, and does it stay understandable for normal people who just want to get work done?
