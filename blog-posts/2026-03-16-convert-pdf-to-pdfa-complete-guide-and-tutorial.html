Understanding Convert PDF to PDF/A

Convert your PDF documents to the ISO-standardized PDF/A format for long-term archiving. This detailed guide will explain everything you need to know about using our free online Convert PDF to PDF/A tool effectively.

Benefits

  • Long-term preservation: PDF/A is the ISO standard for archiving documents electronically.
  • Regulatory compliance: Many industries and governments require PDF/A for official records.
  • Self-contained: All fonts and resources are embedded within the document.

How to Use It

Frequently Asked Questions

What is PDF/A?

PDF/A is a specialized version of PDF designed specifically for long-term digital preservation and archiving of electronic documents.

Is PDF/A the same as regular PDF?

No. PDF/A has additional restrictions (like requiring all fonts to be embedded) to ensure documents remain readable for decades.
