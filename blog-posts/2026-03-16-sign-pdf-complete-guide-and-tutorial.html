Sign PDF Tutorial Guide

Your contract arrived by email as a PDF, but your office wants a signed copy back by tomorrow — and the nearest printer is across town. Or your landlord needs a signed agreement but you are three states away. The Sign PDF tool on PDF For All lets you add your handwritten or typed signature directly to any PDF, free, in any browser, without printing a single page.

What is Sign PDF?

Sign PDF is a free browser-based tool for adding digital signatures to PDF documents. You can draw your signature with your mouse or finger, type it in a cursive font, or upload an image of your handwritten signature. Place it anywhere on the page, resize it, and download the signed PDF — entirely in your browser, with zero cloud storage involved. Try Sign PDF free here — no signup, no installation, works on any device including mobile.

Why You Need Sign PDF (Real-World Use Cases)

Here are the most common situations where the Sign PDF tool saves time for students, professionals, and government employees in India:

  • Offer letters and employment agreements: Sign your offer letter digitally and email it back without printing, scanning, or visiting an office.
  • Rent and lease agreements: Sign rental agreements remotely when you cannot meet your landlord in person.
  • Freelance contracts: Add your signature to client contracts and NDAs instantly, from your phone or laptop.
  • Bank and financial forms: Sign authority letters and declarations needed for bank account changes or investments.
  • Permissions and consent forms: Sign school permission slips, medical consent forms, and club membership documents digitally.

How to Use Sign PDF on PDF For All — Step by Step

Using the Sign PDF tool is straightforward. You do not need to download anything or create an account. Here is the complete process:

  1. Open the Sign PDF tool — no account or download required.
  2. Upload the PDF document you need to sign.
  3. Click Add Signature and choose your signature method: draw, type, or upload an image.
  4. Drag the signature to the correct position on the page and resize as needed.
  5. Click Apply and download the signed PDF.

The entire process typically takes under 60 seconds, even on a slow mobile data connection. Everything runs in your browser — no file is ever uploaded to any server.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Use a stylus on a tablet or phone for the most natural-looking drawn signature.
  • After signing, protect the PDF from further edits using Protect PDF.
  • If you need to redact sensitive information before sending, use Edit PDF first.
  • For multi-party documents, each person should sign their own copy of the PDF and return it.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Placing the signature outside the designated signature box in a form — always zoom in to position accurately.
  • Signing a document without reading it first — digital signatures carry legal weight in India under the IT Act.
  • Using a low-resolution image of your signature — it will look blurry at larger sizes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is a digitally signed PDF legally valid in India?

Electronic signatures have legal recognition in India under the Information Technology Act, 2000. For high-stakes legal documents, check with your legal advisor.

Can I add my signature to multiple pages?

Yes. Add and position the signature on each page individually.

Will recipients be able to edit my signature?

No, the signature is embedded into the PDF. To prevent further changes to the document, use Protect PDF after signing.

Does this work on a phone?

Yes. Draw your signature with your finger on a touchscreen — works well in Chrome and Safari on mobile.
