Extract Pages from PDF Tutorial Guide

Understanding Extract Pages from PDF

Pull out specific pages from a PDF to create a new, smaller document. This detailed guide will explain everything you need to know about using our free online Extract Pages from PDF tool effectively.

Benefits

  • Precise selection: Pick exactly which pages you need from a large document.
  • Zero quality loss: Extracted pages are identical to the originals.
  • Fast processing: Runs entirely in your browser for instant results.

How to Use It

  1. Upload your PDF file.
  2. Select the pages you want to extract using the visual page grid.
  3. Click "Extract Pages" to create a new PDF with only the selected pages.
  4. Download your new PDF.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between Extract and Split?

Extract creates one PDF from your selected pages. Split divides the document into multiple separate files.

Can I extract non-consecutive pages?

Yes, you can click to select any combination of pages from the visual grid.
