Flatten PDF Tutorial Guide

Understanding Flatten PDF

Make all form fields and annotations permanently non-editable. This detailed guide will explain everything you need to know about using our free online Flatten PDF tool effectively.

Benefits

  • Secure documents: Prevents further unauthorized edits to form fields.
  • Instant processing: Happens locally without uploading.

How to Use It

  1. Upload the fillable PDF.
  2. Click "Flatten PDF".
  3. Download your read-only PDF document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can a flattened PDF be un-flattened?

No, flattening is permanent.

Conclusion

Using the Flatten PDF tool is quick, easy, and completely free. Start optimizing your workflow today!
