Add Header & Footer: Complete Guide and Tutorial

By LovePDFs Team
Add Header & Footer Tutorial Guide

Your company requires every internal report to show the department name at the top and 'Confidential' at the bottom of every page. Or you need to stamp a document number and date across a 50-page submission. Doing this manually is impossible without proper software — until now. The Add Header & Footer tool on PDF For All adds custom text to every page of your PDF instantly, free, in any browser.

What is Add Header & Footer?

Add Header & Footer stamps custom text at the top and bottom of every page in a PDF. You can include the document title, company name, date, confidentiality notice, watermark text, or any other information. Choose font, size, alignment, and position. No software or account required. Try Add Header & Footer free here — no signup, no installation, works on mobile.

Why You Need Add Header & Footer (Real-World Use Cases)

  • Company branding: Add organisation name and department to every page of official reports and proposals.
  • Confidentiality notices: Stamp 'CONFIDENTIAL' or 'INTERNAL USE ONLY' in the footer of sensitive documents.
  • Document versioning: Add a document version and date header for document control in regulated industries.
  • Academic submissions: Add student name, roll number, and subject to every page of an assignment PDF.
  • Legal documents: Add case number, client name, and date stamp to every page of court filings.

How to Use Add Header & Footer on PDF For All — Step by Step

Using the Add Header & Footer tool is quick and requires no account or download:

  1. Open the Add Header & Footer tool on PDF For All.
  2. Upload your PDF.
  3. Enter your header text (top of page) and footer text (bottom of page).
  4. Choose alignment: left, centre, or right for each.
  5. Set font, font size, and margin distance.
  6. Click Apply and download.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Combine with Add Page Numbers — add the header/footer first, then overlay page numbers in a different corner.
  • Use a light opacity or small font size for header/footer text to keep it unobtrusive.
  • After adding, flatten with Flatten PDF to lock the header and footer permanently.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Setting font size too large which overlaps with body text near the top or bottom margins.
  • Not checking every page type — some PDFs have pages with very small margins.
  • Adding header/footer before you have finalised page order — organize pages first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I add different text to different pages?

The tool applies the same header and footer to all pages. For per-page control, split the PDF.

Can I use date variables?

You can type the date manually as text. Dynamic variables depend on tool version support.

Does it work on protected PDFs?

Unlock the PDF first with the Unlock PDF tool.

Will the header/footer scale correctly on different page sizes?

Yes — text is placed relative to the page edges regardless of page size.
