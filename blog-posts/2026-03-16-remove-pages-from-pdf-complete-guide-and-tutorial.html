Remove Pages from PDF: Complete Guide and Tutorial

By LovePDFs Team
Remove Pages from PDF Tutorial Guide

Your PDF has blank pages from a print driver, cover pages you no longer need, or confidential sections that should not be shared. Manually recreating the document without those pages takes too long. The Remove Pages from PDF tool on PDF For All lets you delete any pages instantly — free, browser-based, zero installation.

What is Remove Pages from PDF?

Remove Pages from PDF deletes specific pages you select from a PDF and returns the remaining document as a new, clean file. The page numbering is not adjusted automatically — the remaining pages keep their original content. Use this to clean up drafts, remove blank pages, or strip unnecessary sections. Try Remove Pages from PDF free here — no signup, no installation, works on mobile.

Why You Need Remove Pages from PDF (Real-World Use Cases)

  • Removing blank pages: Delete blank pages generated by double-sided printing or scanner settings.
  • Stripping cover pages: Remove old title pages before adding a new branding cover to a reused document.
  • Deleting confidential sections: Remove specific pages before sharing a document externally.
  • Cleaning up merged files: After merging PDFs, delete duplicate or irrelevant pages from the combined document.
  • Trimming scanned batches: Remove bad scans (overexposed, blank, or misread) from a batch-scanned document.

How to Use Remove Pages from PDF on PDF For All — Step by Step

Using the Remove Pages from PDF tool is quick and requires no account or download:

  1. Open the Remove Pages tool on PDF For All.
  2. Upload your PDF.
  3. Click on the pages you want to remove — they will be highlighted.
  4. Click Remove Selected Pages.
  5. Download the cleaned PDF.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Remove blank pages before compressing — compression works better on content-only pages.
  • If removing many pages, use Extract Pages to keep only the pages you want instead.
  • After removing pages, compress with Compress PDF to reduce file size.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Removing the wrong pages — always check page thumbnails before confirming deletion.
  • Not keeping a backup of the original before removing pages.
  • Using this tool on a password-protected PDF — unlock first.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I remove multiple pages at once?

Yes. Select multiple pages before clicking Remove.

Does removing pages update the page numbers?

No. The visible content is unchanged. Use Add Page Numbers after to renumber.

Is there an undo option?

No — keep the original file as a backup before removing pages.

Does it work on mobile?

Yes. Works in Chrome and Safari on Android and iOS browsers.
