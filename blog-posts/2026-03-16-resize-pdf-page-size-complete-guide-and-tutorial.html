Resize PDF Page Size: Complete Guide and Tutorial

By LovePDFs Team
Resize PDF Page Size Tutorial Guide

Your PDF pages are in US Letter size but the printer or the university portal requires A4. Or a designer sent you a PDF in a non-standard dimension and your print shop says it won't fit the press. The Resize PDF Page Size tool on PDF For All converts all pages to any standard size — free, in your browser, no software needed.

What is Resize PDF Page Size?

Resize PDF Page Size changes the paper dimensions of all pages in a PDF to a standard size — A4, US Letter, A3, Legal, and more. Content is scaled proportionally to fit the new page dimensions. Processing is entirely local — no server upload required. Try Resize PDF Page Size free here — no signup, no installation, works on mobile.

Why You Need Resize PDF Page Size (Real-World Use Cases)

  • Standardising for print: Convert Letter-size PDFs to A4 (the standard in India) before sending to a print shop.
  • University portal submissions: Some portals require A4 format — resize before uploading.
  • Mixing page sizes: Standardise a merged PDF where different source files had different page sizes.
  • Poster and large format printing: Resize pages to A3 or larger for poster printing.
  • Reducing or scaling content: Change page dimensions to scale down a presentation formatted for a large screen.

How to Use Resize PDF Page Size on PDF For All — Step by Step

Using the Resize PDF Page Size tool is quick and requires no account or download:

  1. Open the Resize PDF Page tool on PDF For All.
  2. Upload your PDF.
  3. Choose your target page size from the dropdown (e.g. A4, US Letter, A3).
  4. Choose whether to scale content to fit or keep original content size.
  5. Click Resize and download.

All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server.

Tips & Best Practices

  • Scale content proportionally to avoid text going beyond page edges.
  • After resizing, use Compress PDF if the file size increased.
  • For scanned documents, deskew pages first with Deskew PDF before resizing.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not scaling content and having text cut off at new edges.
  • Resizing a landscape document to portrait without rotating first.
  • Forgetting that changing page size may affect the visible margins and layout.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does resizing maintain content quality?

Yes. Content is vector-based or image-based within the PDF. Scaling it proportionally does not reduce quality.

Can I resize only specific pages?

The tool resizes all pages uniformly. For selective resizing, split the PDF first.

What is the most common size in India?

A4 (210 × 297 mm) is the standard paper size used in India for office, legal, and government documents.

Does resizing help reduce file size?

Slightly, if pages are made smaller. For significant size reduction, use Compress PDF.
