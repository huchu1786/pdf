By LovePDFs Team
Rotate PDF Tutorial Guide

Understanding Rotate PDF

Rotate your PDFs the way you need them. Easily change page orientation from portrait to landscape. This detailed guide will explain everything you need to know about using our free online Rotate PDF tool effectively.

Benefits

  • Fix upside-down pages: Easily correct pages that were scanned upside down.
  • Selective rotation: Apply rotation to specific page types (odd/even) with one click.
  • Secure processing: Your documents never leave your browser.

How to Use It

  1. Select or drag and drop your PDF file into the tool.
  2. Choose the rotation angle (90, 180, or 270 degrees).
  3. Select whether to rotate all pages, odd pages, or even pages.
  4. Click "Rotate PDF" and download your corrected document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I rotate just one page?

Currently, the tool rotates pages based on the pattern you select (All, Odd, Even). For single page manipulation, use our Organize PDF tool.

Is the rotation permanent?

Yes, when you download the resultant file, the orientation is permanently changed.

Conclusion

Using the Rotate PDF tool is quick, easy, and completely free. Start optimizing your workflow today!
