Currency Converter FAQ

How does this currency converter work?
It loads rate data for the session and uses that to convert the selected amount from one currency into another instantly.

Does it support major global currencies?
Yes. It includes a broad list of world currencies, including USD, EUR, GBP, INR, JPY, AUD, CAD, AED, SGD, and many more.

Can I use this for travel and invoices?
Yes. It is useful for budgeting trips, comparing international prices, and translating invoice values into another currency.

Why can the converted value change later?
Exchange rates move over time, so the same currency pair may show a slightly different result later in the day or on another date.
