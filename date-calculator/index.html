What this date calculator helps you do

This date calculator combines three practical tasks in one place: counting the number of days between two dates, adding or subtracting days from a selected date, and finding the weekday for any date. That makes it useful for planning deadlines, renewals, travel, event schedules, exam dates, delivery targets, and general calendar math.

Instead of working things out manually, you can get a quick answer and move on. If you are planning across timelines, progress rates, or age-based dates, the Age Calculator, Percentage Calculator, and Loan Calculator are also useful companion tools.

Example use cases

Check how many days remain until an exam, add 45 days to an invoice date for follow-up planning, or find which weekday a birthday or appointment will fall on.

Good for planning

Students, teams, freelancers, and families can all use it to estimate time gaps, schedule milestones, and keep deadlines visible.

Simple date math

The page gives you a clean result without spreadsheet formulas, including days, approximate weeks and months, and the exact target date.

Date Calculator FAQ

How many days between two dates calculator?
Open the Date Difference tab, select the start and end dates, and the tool shows the gap in days with supporting time breakdowns.

Can I add or subtract dates here?
Yes. Use the Add / Subtract Days tab, choose the operation, and enter the number of days to move forward or backward.

Can this tool tell me the weekday for a date?
Yes. The Day of Week tab shows the weekday for any date you select.

Is this useful for deadline planning?
Yes. It is especially helpful for contracts, exams, renewals, project milestones, and event planning.
