Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know — answered in plain English.

Q: Is LovePDFs really free?

Yes! All core tools are 100% free with no sign-up required. We support the service with Google AdSense ads.

Q: Are my files safe?

Completely. All processing happens in your browser using WebAssembly and JavaScript. Your files never leave your device or touch our servers.

Q: Can I use LovePDFs on mobile?

Absolutely. The site is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops in any modern browser.

Q: Is there a file size limit?

There is no enforced server-side limit since files never reach our servers. Very large files may be slower depending on your device's CPU and RAM.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

No account required — just open a tool, drop your file, and click the action button.

Q: What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari (all modern versions). We recommend Chrome for the best performance.

Q: Can I convert between file formats?

Yes! We support PDF to Word, PDF to JPG, JPG to PDF, Word to PDF, HTML to PDF, and more.

Q: How do I compress a PDF?

Go to Compress PDF, upload your file, choose a quality preset (70%, 50%, or 25%), and download the result.

Q: Do you use cookies?

We use minimal essential cookies for theme preferences and history. See our Cookies page for details.

Q: Who made LovePDFs?

LovePDFs was built by an independent developer passionate about free, privacy-first tools. Visit our contact page if you need support or want to share feedback.
