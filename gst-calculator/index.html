What is GST?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax used in India. It replaced multiple cascading taxes and is applied at rates of 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, or 28%.

GST Exclusive vs Inclusive

Exclusive: GST is added on top of the price (e.g., price ₹1000 + 18% GST = ₹1180). Inclusive: GST is already included in the stated price.

CGST + SGST = IGST

For intra-state transactions, GST is split equally into CGST (Central) and SGST (State). For inter-state, it's charged as IGST (Integrated).

GST Calculator FAQ

How is GST calculated on an exclusive amount?
Multiply the amount by the GST rate and add that tax value to the original price.

How do I remove GST from an inclusive amount?
Select the inclusive option and the tool separates the original base amount from the GST portion automatically.

What is the difference between CGST, SGST, and IGST?
CGST and SGST are typically used for intra-state transactions, while IGST applies to inter-state transactions.

Can I use this for both goods and services?
Yes. As long as you know the correct GST slab, the calculator works for both.
