Why Is It Free?

Good question. Here's the honest answer.

⚡
Zero Server Costs
Since all processing happens in your browser, we don't pay for computing servers, bandwidth, or storage. That saving goes straight to you.
❤️
Open Source Roots
We're built on free, open-source libraries (PDF-lib, PDF.js, JSZip). Giving back by making a free tool feels right.
🌍
Accessible for Everyone
PDF tools shouldn't cost $15/month. Students, small businesses, and individuals deserve professional tools without a subscription.
