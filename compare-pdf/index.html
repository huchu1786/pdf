About Compare PDF

Comparing PDFs is useful when you need to review revisions, verify version changes, or check whether important text, formatting, and layout details have shifted between drafts. This page is built to give you the same polished experience as the rest of LovePDFs while keeping the work private in your browser.

Who uses this?

Legal teams compare contracts, students review assignments, businesses verify policy updates, and editors confirm final document revisions.

How it works

The tool reads text from both PDFs, checks wording differences, and also estimates formatting and layout changes so you can review more than plain text.

Private by default

Your files stay on your device. No account, no server-side storage, and no complicated upload workflow to compare two files.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Drop or select your original PDF in the left upload box.
  2. Drop or select the modified PDF in the right upload box.
  3. Configure options (ignore case, spaces, numbers) as needed.
  4. Click "Compare PDFs" - differences appear instantly.
  5. Use Prev/Next to navigate changes, or download the text report.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compare two PDFs online for free?

Upload both PDFs using the drop zones above and click "Compare PDFs". The tool extracts text from each file and highlights every difference for you - no account or server upload required.

Are my files safe? Are they uploaded to a server?

Your files are never uploaded anywhere. All processing happens inside your browser using PDF.js and JavaScript. Your documents stay completely private on your device.

What does Strict Mode do?

In normal mode the tool ignores minor whitespace and punctuation variations. Strict Mode does an exact character-by-character match - every space, comma, and hyphen counts as a potential difference.

Can I compare scanned PDFs?

This page works best with text-based PDFs. If your file is scanned or image-only, use our OCR tool first to extract text and then run the comparison again.

How do I export the comparison results?

After comparison, click "Download Text Report" to save a plain-text file listing all additions and removals together with the similarity percentage.
