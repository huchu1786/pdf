Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our top priority. Here's exactly how we handle your data — in plain English.

🔒

The Short Version

Your files never leave your device. All PDF and image processing happens entirely in your web browser using JavaScript and WebAssembly. We have no servers that receive, store, or process your files.

Why this policy matters

This Privacy Policy explains how LovePDFs handles information in plain language. Our tools are designed to reduce the amount of data we need to collect in the first place, especially for document processing. That makes this page an important companion to the tools, because it tells visitors what happens when they use the service and what does not happen.

Last updated: March 19, 2026.

1. Information We Collect

LovePDFs does not collect personal information. We do not require registration, login, or any account creation.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics (page views, tool clicks) to understand which features are popular. This data contains no personally identifiable information and cannot be linked to you.

2. Your Files & Documents

Your files are processed 100% locally in your browser. When you upload a PDF or image to LovePDFs:

  • The file is loaded into your browser's memory only
  • Processing is done by JavaScript running on your device
  • The file is never transmitted to any server
  • We never see, store, or have access to your file contents
  • Closing the browser tab permanently removes all traces of the file

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage (a browser feature) to store your dark/light mode preference and your file processing history. This data lives only on your device and is never sent to us.

We may use minimal technical cookies for basic website functionality. We do not use tracking, advertising, or third-party analytics cookies.

4. Third-Party Services

LovePDFs loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). This may expose your IP address to Google in accordance with Google's own Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are loaded.

PDF processing libraries (pdf-lib, PDF.js) are loaded from trusted CDN providers (cdnjs.cloudflare.com). These are open-source libraries that run locally in your browser.

5. Data Retention

Because we don't collect your data, there is nothing for us to retain. Your processing history is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage.

6. Children's Privacy

LovePDFs is a general-purpose tool service. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date below and posting a notice on the homepage. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Last updated: March 19, 2026

8. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? We're happy to clarify anything.

Plain-language summary

If you use LovePDFs only for the core browser tools, the key point is simple: we try to avoid collecting sensitive file content at all. If you contact us directly, the information you choose to send may be processed so we can reply and improve the service, but we do not treat personal messages as a data product.
