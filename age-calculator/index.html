How is age calculated?

Age = difference in years, months, and days between your Date of Birth and the reference date. Leap years are handled correctly.

Why use As Of date?

You can calculate age as of any date in the past or future — useful for legal age checks, birthday countdowns, or historical records.

Is it accurate?

Yes. The calculator accounts for leap years and varying month lengths to give the exact years, months, weeks, and days.

About This Age Calculator

This age calculator is designed for people who need more than a rough birthday estimate. It calculates exact age using the difference between a date of birth and a selected reference date, then breaks the result into years, months, weeks, and days. That makes it helpful for forms, records, school admissions, milestone tracking, and general personal planning.

Because the tool supports a custom "as of" date, it can be used for both present and past calculations. You can check your age today, verify age on a specific historical date, or find the exact age that will apply on a future event. Leap years and different month lengths are handled automatically so the result is more precise than a simple year subtraction.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to calculate exact age from date of birth?
Enter your date of birth and choose the reference “as of” date. The calculator then returns your exact age in years, months, and days.
How does the age calculator work?
It compares the date of birth with the selected reference date and calculates the exact difference.
Does it handle leap years?
Yes. Leap years and different month lengths are included in the calculation.
Can I calculate age for a past date?
Yes. The "as of" field lets you calculate age for past or future dates.
Why is this better than just subtracting years?
Simple year subtraction can be off by months or days. This tool gives a full calendar-based result.
